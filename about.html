About Model Guru

Building the Future of AI Model Discovery

Our Mission

At Model Guru, we're on a mission to transform how developers, researchers, and AI enthusiasts discover and track AI models. We believe that the rapid evolution of AI should be accessible and manageable for everyone.

Our platform serves as a central hub, aggregating and analyzing models from leading platforms like Hugging Face, Replicate, and Stability AI. We track key metrics such as usage patterns, community engagement, and model performance to help you make informed decisions.
